## Arrears - The 1% Solution:

We offer a one percent solution on arrears. The conditions are:

At the end of a twelve-month fiscal period, we will assure the Corporation that the arrears are one per cent (or less) of the budgeted annual condominium fees.  
This solution does not apply to other income or special assessments.  
Collections will be implemented in accordance with the Act and the Corporation’s by-laws.
